Counselling & Psychosocial Support

Trauma-informed, confidential counselling for survivors — individual, family, and group sessions.

How We Help

Our Counselling Services

Individual Counselling

One-on-one trauma-focused support with trained counsellors to process experiences and build coping strategies.

Group Therapy & Peer Support

Safe group spaces to share, connect and heal with facilitated sessions and peer-led groups.

Family & Child Support

Child-friendly services and family counselling to support recovery and safety planning.

Referral Network

We coordinate referrals to specialised mental health services, psychiatric care, and long-term therapy where needed.
